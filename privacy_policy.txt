PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: February 05, 2026

Employee Suite ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Shopify application "Employee Suite".

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly to us and information about your use of the application.

Personal Information:
- Contact information (name, email address) provided during account creation or support requests.
- Shopify store details (store URL, shop owner name, email, timezone).

Store Data:
We access the following data from your Shopify store as necessary to provide our services:
- Products and Inventory: To analyze stock levels and predict stockouts.
- Orders: To analyze sales trends and revenue for reporting.
- Customers: Access is limited to necessary order fulfillment data and is processed securely.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- Analyze trends and usage to provide inventory and revenue insights.
- Send technical notices, updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.
- Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests.

3. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information and store data only as long as necessary to provide the Service, or as required by law.
- Upon uninstallation, we will remove your store access token.
- Historical data reports may be retained for a limited period to allow for re-installation recovery, or deleted immediately upon request.

4. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with third-party vendors who allow us to provide the Service (e.g., hosting providers like Render, email services like SendGrid), solely for the purpose of enabling them to provide services to us.

5. SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption of access tokens and secure HTTPS connections, to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal information. You may contact us at the email below to exercise these rights.
